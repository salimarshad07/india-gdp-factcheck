🔍 **I fact-checked a viral chart that reached millions — and the numbers don't quite add up.**

You've probably seen it. Bold orange bar. "UNSTOPPABLE INDIA." 7.7% GDP growth. Flags of 18 countries. "Source: MoSPI, Media Reports."

It went viral across WhatsApp, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn. Shared by politicians, news anchors, and proud citizens.

So I ran the numbers against IMF data.

**Here's what I found:**

📊 India's actual IMF-verified growth rate for 2025: **7.618%** (not 7.7%)
🇺🇸 USA's claimed figure: 2.8% — IMF says: **2.117%** — gap of +0.68%
🇧🇷 Brazil's claimed figure: 3.5% — IMF says: **2.286%** — gap of +1.21%

The viral chart wasn't lying about India being the fastest-growing major economy. **That part is true.**

But it mixed IMF forecasts with MoSPI estimates without disclosing the methodology, and several countries' figures were significantly overstated — especially USA and Brazil.

**India's growth story is real. It doesn't need embellishment.**
